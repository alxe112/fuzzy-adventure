The Nerdverse: Aetherfall Vortex

Epic Legendary Campaign

A single-file RPG adventure set in Aetherfall: a world born from Lumina and Umbros, scarred by the Sundering Wars, and now sliding toward the Shadow War. You begin at a game-night frame, but the world quickly expands into kingdoms, cults, arenas, haunted ruins, living forests, legendary artifacts, and a final choice that can save the world or corrupt it forever.

Character Rules

You select one of the 12 D&D classes, then distribute exactly 16 points across Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.

Base score is 10. Class bonuses apply on top. These affect quests, combat, loot, crafting, branching choices, and major endings.
